Meet Our Founder

  • Roshan Singh L

    Founder

    Born on October 28, 1993, is a highly skilled and dedicated professional in the field of health, fitness, and personal development. With over 11 years of experience, Roshan has established himself as a leading Health Coach, Life Coach, and Wellness Coach.


    He is also the proud founder of AUM THE FACILITOR, an organization committed to empowering individuals to achieve their health and wellness goals. Throughout his career, Roshan has worked with renowned health and fitness facilities, including Gold's Gym and F45 Training. His expertise lies in helping clients reach their goals, preventing injuries, and guiding them towards adopting a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Roshan's professional credibility is reinforced by an impressive array of qualifications and certifications. He is a Certified Functional Trainer, Certified Personal Trainer, and Certified Group Coach, demonstrating his expertise in designing effective and personalized workout programs. Recognizing the significance of nutrition in overall well-being, Roshan holds diplomas in Nutrition, Yogic Nutrition, and Sports Nutrition. These qualifications enable him to provide comprehensive guidance on dietary planning and nutritional strategies tailored to individual needs. Roshan's commitment to holistic wellness is further evidenced by his certifications as a Life Coach, Holistic Health Coach, Wellness Coach, and Master Mindfulness Coach. He skillfully combines various modalities to empower individuals to enhance their physical, mental, and emotional well-being. In addition to his fitness and wellness qualifications, Roshan has pursued diplomas in Yoga, Meditation, Reiki, Acupressure, Color Therapy, Counselling, Psychotherapy, ADHD Management, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Hypnotherapy, and NLP Life Coaching. These diverse areas of expertise allow him to provide comprehensive support and guidance to his clients. Roshan's clients have consistently praised his expertise and dedication. Positive reviews highlight his ability to provide close attention, engaging workout routines, and effective post-workout stretches that minimize muscle pain. Clients also appreciate his motivation, knowledge, and the excellent results they achieve under his guidance. With his vibrant energy and perfect technique, Roshan has earned a reputation as one of the best instructors in Bengaluru. His dedication and encouragement inspire clients to push their boundaries and strive for continuous improvement. As a Health Coach, Life Coach, and Wellness Coach, Roshan customizes exercise programs to suit individual needs, ensuring that each client receives personalized attention and achieves their desired outcomes. If you are seeking a highly skilled and passionate professional to guide you on your health and wellness journey, Roshan Singh L is an excellent choice. His extensive qualifications, glowing testimonials, and commitment to client success make him a trusted facilitator in the realm of holistic well-being.
